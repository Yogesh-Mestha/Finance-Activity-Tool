Jeevan Umang is a non-linked, with-profits, whole life insurance policy offered by the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC). It is designed to provide life insurance coverage and regular income benefits to the policyholder for their entire lifetime

Mutual Fund

Equity Fund

An equity fund is a type of mutual fund that primarily invests in stocks or shares of companies listed on the stock exchange. The objective of an equity fund is to provide capital appreciation to its investors by investing in stocks of companies with growth potential.

Debt Fund

A debt fund is a type of mutual fund that invests primarily in fixed income securities such as bonds, treasury bills, and other debt securities.

Public Provident Fund (PPF) account is one of the most popular long term investment options for individuals with low-risk appetite as both the money in the PPF account and the returns it generates are guaranteed
